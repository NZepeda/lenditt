Lenditt
RENT, EARN, DISCOVER.
Lenditt is a peer-to-peer rental marketplace providing an outlet for anyone to rent out the things they own, while allowing others to rent the things they need.
RENT
Rent the things you need with Lenditt!
In need of a Drone for a film project? Or are you in the market for a drone, but aren’t ready to commit to the purchase? With Lenditt, rent the Drone you need to finish your film project or take it for a spin before committing.
EARN
Earn with Lenditt by sharing your unused belongings!
Not only can you earn money by listing your dust-collecting belongings on our marketplace, but Lenditt helps you pay off any new purchases by

sharing them with others!

DISCOVER
Disover and explore new hobbies with Lenditt!
Looking to go camping with friends for the first time? Learning how to play guitar? With Lenditt, you can discover new hobbies and explore many possibilities by booking your interests through our rental marketplace.

It’s that simple.
