Put Your Things To Work

With Lenditt, turn your belongings into assets by sharing them with others.

Share your things

Do you have a bicycle at home collecting dust? Rent it out for others to enjoy! Going camping and need a tent? Search local listings and rent what you need. Once your trip is over, simply return it and be on your way! No extra baggage required!

Earn Some Money

Repurpose those dust collecting belongings and send them out into the world to fulfill their destiny! (Their money collecting destiny, that is…) By renting out your things you can sit back, relax, and enjoy your day while they are hard at work collecting the rest of the cash needed for your share of the utilities!

Do much more

Having a last minute party and forgot to rent that karaoke set-up your roommate told you NOT to forget? Browse nearby listings to find your missing karaoke machine and get the party started! Looking to buy a shiny new projector to host movie nights with your friends, but aren’t’ really looking to spend that kind of money? Make the investment and send your projector out to work! Lenditt to others and watch it pay for itself! See what we did there?
